About Me

I am Veger Bernhard Pena Cruz. I was born in Queens, New York. I spent nine years in New York before I moved to Pennsylvania.

After I graduated from high school, I joined the United States Marine Corps. I spent four years on active duty, before I retired.

After I left active duty service I joined the Marine Corps reserve. I spent eight months in Diesel technician school.

Once I graduated from school, I was hired to work at Jaguar Land Rover, Manhattan.
